KaraokeSpin

Are you ready for a great new game for karaoke night? This will keep your club, bar, restaurant, whatever hopping and having a great time.

Take a Spin

How it Works

Step 1: Add your singers to the queue

Simply add your singers to the queue and hit "spin".

Step 2: Choose from 12 random karaoke songs

Each singer will be given a selection of 12 YouTube karaoke videos chosen at random.

Step 3: Sing your heart out!

Choose a song from the list and start singing. If you can't decide, take another spin or let the crowd choose for you.
